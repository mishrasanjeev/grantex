What Grantex does

Authority

For allowlisted AgenticOrg tenants, the implemented authority route issues or refuses canonical internal OACP artifacts.

Policy

Defines source, freshness, TTL, revocation, and risk rules.

Verification

Checks issuer, signature metadata, payload hash, scope, and safety.

Adapters

Governs compatibility mappings for client and partner payloads.

Why Grantex is not a toll booth

Grantex signs and verifies authority artifacts. AgenticOrg may answer non-binding buyer questions from valid cached artifacts until TTL, freshness, revocation, or policy state requires refresh.

Payment, POS, and order boundary

Final payment, mandate, checkout, POS confirmation, order, stock hold, refund, return, and shipment execution requires the approved provider, merchant, POS, and channel path. OACP artifacts do not invent success.

Integrate AgenticOrg with Grantex authority

AgenticOrg sends Grantex public-safe authority requests for allowlisted tenants, caches returned OACP artifacts, and exposes buyer surfaces with source and freshness labels.
