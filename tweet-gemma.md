New: @grantex/gemma — offline-first auth for edge AI agents.

On-device agents (Google Gemma, local LLMs) can't phone home to verify permissions. So we built consent bundles: fetch once while online, verify forever offline.

AES-256-GCM encrypted, RS256 JWT verified, Ed25519 signed audit chain. Syncs back when connectivity returns. Works with Google ADK and LangChain out of the box.
